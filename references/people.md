# Role Lenses

Use this file when the task is not just about a group context, but about how a specific collaborator or approval role tends to think, structure a proposal, or drive review outcomes.

## Top-Level Approver

Durable signals:

- gives short, decisive corrections around shape, proportion, realism, color stability, and transition semantics
- prefers designs that feel more accurate, more restrained, and less likely to be misunderstood
- notices when an effect or transition could create the wrong product meaning, not just when it looks visually odd
- quickly narrows the direction rather than exploring many speculative branches

How to apply this lens:

- lead with what became clearer, more stable, or less misleading
- prioritize shape, proportion, color comfort, and whether the interaction meaning reads correctly
- avoid over-selling decorative novelty if the core form still feels off

Suggested output shape:

- `这版哪里更顺`
- `哪里更像真实物或合理结构`
- `哪里减少了误解风险`
- `还剩哪个细节要继续收紧`

## Direction Amplifier

Durable signals:

- amplifies promising ideas by asking for stronger immersion, motion, atmosphere, or a more showable 2.0 version
- likes designs that have亮点 and spread potential, not just baseline correctness
- pays attention to whether a good case can be shared, showcased, or learned from across teams
- encourages, but usually points toward a specific higher ceiling

How to apply this lens:

- do not stop at `稳`; add one step about how the idea can become more immersive, memorable, or shareable
- offer a reasonable upgrade path such as `2.0`, motion enhancement, or broader rollout possibility
- connect design work to visibility, learning value, or cross-team influence when relevant

Suggested output shape:

- `当前亮点是什么`
- `还能往哪一步放大`
- `2.0 版本可以增强什么`
- `为什么值得更大范围亮相或复用`

## System Builder

Durable signals:

- works closer to design strategy, data validation, and reporting readiness than to pure visual expression
- asks whether a design judgment has enough evidence, not just whether the output looks reasonable
- is comfortable marking a conclusion as uncertain and waiting for more data instead of overstating confidence
- treats a single case as insufficient proof; repeated cases and practical validation matter
- tends to open with background and rationale, then explain the current tactic, then mention implementation or risk handling
- turns unstable constraints into design-system or process-level mitigations

How to apply this lens:

- separate what problem is being solved, what evidence threshold is being used, how the proposal becomes reusable, and how it becomes easier to report upward
- do not reduce the approach to surface style words alone
- prefer phrasing such as `先讲背景依据`, `再讲当前解法`, `最后讲评估和落地`

Suggested output shape:

- `在解决什么问题`
- `如何组织判断依据`
- `如何控制结论强度`
- `如何为汇报和拍板做准备`
